My hobby is football, I love everything about football. I love watching it and I play it too, it has always been my hobby since I was a child. I remember school days, how I will stay back after school dismissal, to play football with my friends in the school field.
Honestly, football makes me happy. Watching my favorite team win their match is a thing of joy. Playing football increases my confidence and self-esteem.
I have traveled to Anambra state and Aba to play football for my team. I led my school team as a captain in a football competition in Umuahia, I don't think I will experience that again.
